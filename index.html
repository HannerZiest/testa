      What this can and can't do. This monitors signals available to a web page: tab/visibility changes, window focus, fullscreen exit, copy/paste/cut, right-click, and printing. It is a soft integrity aid, not proof of misconduct — a brief focus loss can be innocent, and it cannot see other devices, browser extensions, or overlays that never take focus. Catching those requires monitoring outside the browser (a lockdown app or camera-based proctoring). Test-takers should always be told this monitoring is active.
